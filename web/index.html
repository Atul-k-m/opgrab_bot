ℹ️ About OpportunityIQ

The vision, architecture, and commands behind the intelligence platform

Our Mission

OpportunityIQ was created to bridge the gap between talented individuals and the opportunities that match their skills. Our AI-driven platform cuts through the noise of thousands of generic job postings to deliver personalized, high-signal recommendations.

⚙️ How It Works

1

Multi-Source Ingestion

Every 6 hours, our scraper autonomously fetches the latest opportunities from Unstop, Devfolio, Internshala, Remotive, Arbeitnow, and LinkedIn. It actively filters out irrelevant senior and PhD roles.

2

Intelligent AI Matching

Powered by Groq (llama-3.3-70b), the bot scores each opportunity (0-100) based on your personalized profile, taking into account your batch year, skills, domains, and preference for remote or India-accessible roles.

3

Seamless Delivery

The best matches are delivered right to your Telegram chat, complete with AI-generated reasoning on exactly why an opportunity fits you.

🤖 Telegram Bot Commands

Interact directly with the intelligence engine through our Telegram bot. Start by sending /start to build your AI profile.

/start Launch the onboarding flow to set your Batch, Domains, Skills, and CGPA.
/foryou Get the top AI-ranked opportunities specifically tailored to your profile.
/internships Discover the latest internships, heavily ranked towards India/Remote roles.
/jobs Find entry-level tech roles and remote positions globally.
/hackathons Explore upcoming hackathons worldwide.
/profile View your current skills, domain tracking coverage, and match settings.
/refresh Force the scraper to run immediately to find newly posted roles.
